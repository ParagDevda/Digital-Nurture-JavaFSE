Sorting Customer Orders


1. Aim
    To sort customer orders based on their total price using Bubble Sort and Quick Sort and compare their performance.

2. Understand Sorting Algorithms
    Sorting is the process of arranging data in ascending or descending order.
     It improves searching efficiency and helps prioritize high-value orders.

Bubble Sort
     Compares adjacent elements.
     Swaps them if they are in the wrong order.
     Repeats until the array becomes sorted.

  Advantages
    Simple to implement.

  Disadvantages
    Slow for large datasets.

Quick Sort
    Uses Divide and Conquer strategy.
    Selects a pivot element.
    Partitions the array into smaller subarrays.
    Recursively sorts the subarrays.

Advantages
    Very efficient.
    Faster than Bubble Sort for large datasets.
